How to Acquire $TB

01

Create a Wallet

Download a crypto wallet like MetaMask or Trust Wallet from the app store or browser extension store for free.

02

Get Some ETH

Have ETH in your wallet to swap for $TB. If you don't have any, you can buy directly on MetaMask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on an exchange.

03

Go to Uniswap

Connect to Uniswap. Go to app.uniswap.org in your browser or within your wallet's browser. Connect your wallet.

04

Swap for $TB

Paste the $TB contract address into Uniswap, select $TB, and confirm. When MetaMask prompts, sign the transaction.
